Factors & Multiples: The Secret Garden

After solving the treasure chest puzzle, Maya and Arjun found themselves in a beautiful garden where flowers were arranged in perfect rectangular patterns. The Garden Keeper challenged them to understand the secret behind these arrangements.

Maya: These 24 roses are arranged in rows and columns!

Arjun: Let's find all the ways we can arrange them in rectangles!

Problem: Find all factor pairs of 24
Solution:
Factor pairs of 24: (1,24), (2,12), (3,8), (4,6)
So we can arrange 24 roses in 4 different rectangular patterns!

Ratio & Proportion: The Cooking Challenge

The garden adventure led Maya and Arjun to the Royal Kitchen, where the Head Chef was preparing for a grand feast. She needed help adjusting her recipes for different numbers of guests using ratios and proportions.

Maya: The recipe says 2 cups flour for 3 cups sugar!

Arjun: But we need to make it for 15 cups of sugar instead!

Problem: If 2 cups flour : 3 cups sugar, how much flour for 15 cups sugar?
Solution:
Ratio = 2:3, Scale factor = 15√∑3 = 5
Required flour = 2 √ó 5 = 10 cups
