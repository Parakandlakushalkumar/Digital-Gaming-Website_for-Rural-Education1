📒 Syllabus & Quick Notes (Class 11 Maths)

Concise pointers for revision. Expand each topic in your story as needed.

Sets, Relations & Functions

  • Operations on sets: ∪, ∩, ⧵, complement
  • Relation: subset of A×B; Function: every x∈A maps to unique y∈B
  • Types: one‑one, onto, bijection; domain, co‑domain, range

Complex Numbers

  • z = a + bi, i² = −1; |z| = √(a²+b²), arg(z)
  • Conjugate: z̄ = a − bi; z·z̄ = |z|²
  • Algebra: addition, multiplication, polar form (r(cosθ+i sinθ))

Sequences & Series

  • AP: aₙ = a₁+(n−1)d; Sₙ = n/2[2a₁+(n−1)d]
  • GP: aₙ = a₁·rⁿ⁻¹; Sₙ = a₁(1−rⁿ)/(1−r), |r|≠1; S∞ = a₁/(1−r)

Binomial Theorem

  • (a+b)ⁿ = Σ C(n,k)aⁿ⁻ᵏ bᵏ
  • Middle term(s) and greatest term ideas

Coordinate Geometry

  • Straight line: slope m, y=mx+c, point‑slope form
  • Conics: circle x²+y²=r²; parabola, ellipse, hyperbola (standard forms)

Probability & Statistics

  • P(A)∈[0,1], P(A∪B)=P(A)+P(B)−P(A∩B)
  • Mean, median, mode; variance σ², standard deviation σ
