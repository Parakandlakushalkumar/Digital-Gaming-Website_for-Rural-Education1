üç≠ Factor Frenzy Instructions

  • Objective: Find all factors of the target number
  • How to Play: Click on numbers that divide the target evenly
  • Correct Match: The tile turns green and disappears
  • Wrong Match: The tile turns red and locks temporarily
  • Win Condition: Remove all factor tiles from the grid
  • Tip: A factor divides the target number with no remainder

üè∞ Prime Defender Instructions

  • Wrong Answer: Monsters move faster toward your castle
  • Game Over: If a monster reaches your castle
