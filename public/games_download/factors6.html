🍭 Factor Frenzy Instructions

  • Objective: Find all factors of the target number
  • How to Play: Click on numbers that divide the target evenly
  • Correct Match: The tile turns green and disappears
  • Wrong Match: The tile turns red and locks temporarily
  • Win Condition: Remove all factor tiles from the grid
  • Tip: A factor divides the target number with no remainder

🏃‍♂️ Multiple Dash Instructions

  • Objective: Collect multiples of the base number
  • How to Play: Click on number tokens as they appear
  • Correct Collection: +10 points for each multiple
  • Wrong Collection: -5 points for non-multiples
  • Challenge: Tokens appear randomly and move quickly
  • Tip: A multiple is the base number multiplied by any whole number

🏰 Prime Defender Instructions

  • Objective: Defend your castle from monsters
  • How to Play: Answer if the shown number is prime
  • Prime Number: A number greater than 1 with no divisors other than 1 and itself
  • Correct Answer: Destroys the nearest monster
  • Wrong Answer: Monsters move faster toward your castle
  • Game Over: If a monster reaches your castle

🧙‍♂️ Factor Quest Instructions

  • Objective: Unlock all treasure doors
  • How to Play: Click on doors to solve factor puzzles
  • Puzzle: Choose all factors of the given number from the options
  • Correct Choice: Option turns green, door opens with treasure
  • Wrong Choice: Option turns red, try again
  • Victory: Unlock all 4 doors to complete the quest
